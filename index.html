Sign In to your ESPN Account

  • Visit ESPN.com.
  • Click on the "Sign In" button located at the top right corner of the page.
  • Enter your login credentials (email and password) associated with your ESPN subscription.


How to activate ESPN?
  • Go to (espn.com/activate): Open a web browser and navigate to the ESPN activation page.
  • Select Your Device: Choose your device from the list (e.g., Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, etc.).
  • Enter Activation Code: Type in the activation code displayed on your device.
  • Sign In: Log in with your ESPN account or create a new one.
  • Follow Prompts: Complete the activation process by following the on-screen instructions.

Alternative Activation Methods:

  • Mobile App: Download the ESPN app on your mobile device, sign in, and follow the activation prompts.
  • TV Provider: Activate ESPN through your TV provider's website or app (e.g., Xfinity, DirecTV, etc.).

Troubleshooting Activation Issues

While activating your ESPN account is generally straightforward, you may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some troubleshooting tips:

  • Ensure Your Subscription is Active: Double-check that your ESPN+ subscription is active and hasn’t expired.
  • Confirm Login Credentials: Ensure that you are entering the correct email and password. Reset your password if you can’t remember it.
  • Check Internet Connection: Make sure your device has a stable internet connection for the activation process.
  • Try Different Browsers or Devices: If you’re experiencing issues, switching to a different web browser or device may help.
